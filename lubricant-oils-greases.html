Lubricant Oils & Greases

To complement our high-quality hydraulic tubes and fittings, we're pleased to offer a comprehensive range of premium lubricant oils and greases. Proper lubrication is essential for maximizing the lifespan and performance of hydraulic systems. Our selection includes oils and greases formulated to reduce friction, prevent wear, and protect against corrosion, ensuring your hydraulic equipment operates smoothly and reliably. We provide lubricants for various hydraulic applications, from standard operating conditions to extreme temperatures and pressures.

  • Reduces friction and wear for longer equipment life
  • Protects against corrosion, rust, and thermal breakdown
  • Performs reliably under extreme pressure and temperature

Range of Products

Gear Oils

High-performance oils specially engineered to ensure smooth and efficient gear transmission, even under extreme pressure, high loads, and fluctuating temperatures.

Hydraulic Oils

Engineered to deliver reliable and efficient performance in a wide range of hydraulic systems, these oils offer excellent protection against wear, corrosion, and oxidation.

General Purpose Machinery Oils

Specifically formulated to eliminate stick-slip issues and ensure smooth, precise movement of sliding surfaces in machine tools. These oils create a strong lubricating film

Machine Tool Way Oils

Specially developed to reduce stick-slip and ensure smooth, controlled movement on the sliding surfaces of machine tools. These oils form a strong, adhesive lubricating film that resists being wiped away

Metal Cutting, Straight Cutting

We offer precision metal cutting and straight cutting services tailored to meet diverse industrial needs. Using advanced cutting tools and machinery

Rust Preventive Oils

Rust preventive oils are specially formulated lubricants designed to protect metal surfaces from oxidation, corrosion, and rust during storage, transport, or idle conditions

Metal working quenching Oils

Quenching oils are specialized fluids used in the heat treatment of metal parts to rapidly cool and harden them after heating.

High Temperature Greases

High temperature greases are advanced lubricants formulated to perform reliably in extreme heat conditions, where conventional greases would degrade or evaporate
